JS Catalyst is a freelance business development and sales service focused on supporting companies in the mobility, event, and service industries. The core objective is to generate revenue for clients through structured sales activities, client acquisition, and account growth.
The business operates on a performance-based model, combining commission on generated revenue with selective hourly services. This ensures alignment with client success while allowing scalable income growth.
Initial market validation exists through ongoing discussions with potential clients. However, due to industry-specific sales cycles, revenue generation will develop over time. The start-up grant will provide financial stability during this ramp-up phase.

2.1 Personal Motivation & Reason for Self-Employment
Over the past years, I have gained extensive experience in sales and account management and have consistently been driven by performance, results, and building relationships. However, I have also realized that traditional employment structures limit both my productivity and my potential.
I perform best in a results-driven environment where I can focus on outcomes rather than fixed working hours. The ability to work flexibly, manage my own schedule, and take ownership of my results allows me to operate at a significantly higher level of motivation and effectiveness.
In previous roles, I experienced a clear gap between my contribution to revenue and the level of autonomy and financial upside available within a fixed salary structure. This has led me to pursue self-employment, where my performance is directly linked to my results.
Additionally, I am highly motivated by variety, new challenges, and working across different clients and projects. This dynamic environment allows me to stay engaged, continuously develop my skills, and deliver stronger results.
My goal is to build a sustainable business where I can fully apply my strengths in sales and business development, create measurable value for clients, and achieve both professional fulfillment and financial independence.

•	Business development and active sales execution
•	B2B client acquisition and pipeline development
•	Account management and revenue growth

•	Deep industry expertise in mobility, events, and service operations
